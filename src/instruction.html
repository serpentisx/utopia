Instruction

Utopia is known for having magical objects which can be used to heal people

It is a strange place which was taken over by an evil witch

The knight must collect all the tokens in order to save his friend who is heavily wounded

However, there are evil forces anywhere

Use "w" to jump, "a" and "d" to move back and forth

To kill an evil, use "space" to attack

Note that you might have to attack more than once to kill an evil

Be safe and good luck on your journey !
